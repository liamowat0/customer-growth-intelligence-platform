# Business Problem

An e-commerce retailer wants to better understand customer behavior, operational performance, and revenue drivers.

The business currently lacks visibility into:

- Customer retention patterns
- High-value customer segments
- Product category performance
- Fulfillment bottlenecks
- Geographic performance differences
- Customer satisfaction drivers

The goal of this project is to design a business intelligence platform that provides actionable insights through SQL analysis, Excel reporting, and Tableau dashboards.

Key business questions include:

- Which customers generate the most value?
- Which customers are at risk of churning?
- Which product categories drive growth?
- How does fulfillment performance impact customer satisfaction?
- Which operational bottlenecks should be prioritized?
